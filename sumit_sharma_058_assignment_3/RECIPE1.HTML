Paneer Butter Masala

Home

This dish is known for its creamy texture and mildly sweet flavor, making it a favorite in many households.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Spices (garam masala, chili powder, salt)

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add tomato puree.
  2. Add spices and cook until oil separates.
  3. Add paneer cubes and simmer.
  4. Stir in cream and cook for 5 minutes.

Time: 30 mins | Difficulty: Easy
