Biryani

Home

Biryani is a celebration dish, known for its aroma and layers of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Chicken or vegetables
  • Onions, yogurt, spices

Steps

  1. Cook rice separately.
  2. Prepare spiced gravy.
  3. Layer rice and gravy.
  4. Cook on low heat (dum cooking).

Time: 60 mins | Difficulty: Medium
